# grill-me

Interview Kamil relentlessly about the decision until you both reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies one at a time.

- **Ask one question at a time.** Wait for the answer before asking the next. Multiple questions at once is bewildering.
- **For every question, give your recommended answer first.** Kamil is reacting to a pick, not generating one from nothing.
- **Facts get looked up, not asked.** If something can be checked (docs, the codebase, current library versions), go check it — don't put it to him as a question. Only genuine decisions go to him.
- **Do not act until he confirms.** This skill produces a shared understanding, not code.
